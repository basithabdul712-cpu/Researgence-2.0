API Functionality Overview

Each listed repository provides specific functionalities through their APIs:

  • PubMed, Europe PMC: These medical databases offer extensive data on publications and clinical trials, suitable for medical and life sciences research.
  • CrossRef, IEEE Xplore, OpenAlex: These provide broad coverage across multiple disciplines with access to a wide variety of documents including journals, conference proceedings, books, and detailed metadata on authors and institutions.
  • arXiv: Ideal for early access to research in physics, mathematics, computer science, and more through preprints.
  • USPTO, Espacenet, WIPO: Focus on patents and related documents, essential for studies in technology and innovation.
  • ORCID: Integrates personal researcher profiles which can include a wide range of outputs like publications, patents, and project grants.

Researgence: Frequency of Updates

Preamble Researgence is at the forefront of leveraging the vast potential of open access sources to enhance research visibility and accessibility. By expanding the capabilities of these resources, Researgence provides a comprehensive and dynamic platform that continuously adapts and updates to reflect the most current research outputs and metrics. This document outlines the frequency and methodology of updates across various elements within the Researgence system.

  1. Daily Updates of Publications

    Our Daily Feeder System is a robust mechanism that scans and updates publications every day. It processes information from over 110 million metadata entries housed in our proprietary search engine 'Episteme'. This ensures that the latest research publications are readily available to users, maintaining the platform's relevance and utility.

  2. Monthly Updates of Indexing and Citations

    Indexing and citations are critical for the academic visibility of publications. Researgence updates these metrics once a month, drawing from a diverse range of open access information sources. This monthly update cycle helps researchers and institutions keep track of citation metrics and the evolving impact of their work.

  3. Annual and Biannual Updates of Quality Scores

    The quality scores within Researgence that reflects the prestige and credibility of research outputs, are updated annually and biannually. These updates coincide with the release schedules of respective agencies that disseminate this information through open channels. This schedule ensures that the quality scores in Researgence are always aligned with the latest assessments from authoritative bodies.

  4. Rapid Updates of Faculty Contributions

    Faculty contributions to publications and patents are a key component of Researgence. These contributions are validated and updated within 2 business days of submission. This swift update process supports the accuracy of faculty profiles and their associated research outputs, facilitating prompt recognition and reflection of academic achievements.

Conclusion:

The update frequencies outlined above ensure that Researgence remains a cutting-edge platform for research management and dissemination. By continuously integrating the latest data from open access sources, Researgence supports its mission to foster a dynamic and collaborative research ecosystem that is both current and comprehensive.
